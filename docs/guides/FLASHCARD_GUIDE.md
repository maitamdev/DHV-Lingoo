# Flashcard User Guide

## Daily Cards
Every day you receive 5 mystery bags containing vocabulary flashcards.

## Opening Bags
Tap on a mystery bag to reveal the flashcard inside.

## Card Rarities
Cards come in 5 rarities with different XP values:
- Common (grey) - 2 XP
- Uncommon (green) - 3 XP  
- Rare (blue) - 5 XP
- Epic (purple) - 8 XP
- Legendary (gold) - 15 XP

## Flipping Cards
Tap on a revealed card to flip between English and Vietnamese.

## Daily Reset
New cards appear at midnight. Come back every day for fresh vocabulary!
